A Discovery That Changed Everything

In 2005, Bosnian-American archaeologist Dr. Semir Osmanagić identified the hills surrounding Visoko as possibly the largest pyramid complex in the world. What appeared to be natural hills showed striking geometric shapes, unusual stone plates, and an extensive network of underground tunnels.

The Pyramid of the Sun stands 220 metres tall — if confirmed as man-made, it would be larger than the Great Pyramid of Giza. Whether ancient wonder or natural formation, the site draws tens of thousands of visitors every year.
