Travel Guide

Getting Here

Visoko is just 30 km from Sarajevo — easy to reach by car, bus, or guided tour.

🚗

By Car from Sarajevo

~30 km ~35 min

Take the M17 motorway from Sarajevo towards Zenica — Visoko exits are clearly signposted. Free parking is available near the Pyramid of the Sun entrance and the Ravne Tunnels.

Tip: Arrive before 9 AM on weekends in summer to avoid crowds.
🚌

By Bus from Sarajevo

~50 min Very affordable

Regular buses depart from Sarajevo's East Bus Station throughout the day. Buses stop in central Visoko, from where it's a short walk or taxi ride to the pyramid entrances.

  • 🕐 Buses every 30–60 min during the day
  • 💶 Ticket: approx. 4–6 BAM (€2–3)
  • 🚶 Walk from Visoko centre to Sun Pyramid: ~20 min
🧭

Guided Day Tour from Sarajevo

Recommended Full day

Many Sarajevo-based operators offer day trips covering hotel pick-up, transport, a guided tour of both pyramids and tunnels, and return — all for €25–50 per person.

  • ✅ Transport included
  • ✅ Local guide with historical context
  • ✅ Covers pyramids and Ravne tunnels
  • ✅ Available in English and other languages
✈️

From Further Afield

The nearest international airport is Sarajevo International (SJJ), with direct flights from Vienna, Istanbul, Frankfurt, Amsterdam, Zurich and more. From the airport, take a taxi or bus to Sarajevo centre, then connect to Visoko.
