Getting Here

Visoko is easy to reach — it's just 30 km from Sarajevo and well connected by road and public transport.

🚗

By Car from Sarajevo

~30 km ~30–40 minutes

The quickest and most flexible way. Take the M17 motorway from Sarajevo towards Zenica — Visoko exits are clearly signposted. Free parking is available near the Pyramid of the Sun entrance and the Ravne Tunnels.

Tip: Arrive before 9 AM on weekends to avoid crowds in summer.
🚌

By Bus from Sarajevo

~45–60 minutes Very affordable

Regular buses depart from Sarajevo's East Bus Station (Istočno Sarajevo) and the main bus station throughout the day. Buses stop in central Visoko, from where you can walk or take a short taxi to the pyramid entrances.

  • 🕐 Buses run roughly every 30–60 minutes during the day
  • 💶 Ticket: approximately 4–6 BAM (€2–3)
  • 🚶 Walk from Visoko centre to Pyramid of the Sun: ~20 minutes
🧭

Guided Day Tour from Sarajevo

Recommended for first-timers Full day

Many Sarajevo-based tour operators offer day trips to the Bosnian Pyramids. These typically include hotel pick-up, transport, a guided tour of the pyramids and tunnels, and return to Sarajevo — all for €25–50 per person. Highly recommended if it's your first visit.

  • ✅ Transport included
  • ✅ Local guide with historical context
  • ✅ Covers both pyramids and Ravne tunnels
  • ✅ Available in English, German, and other languages
✈️

From Further Afield

The nearest international airport is Sarajevo International Airport (SJJ), with direct flights from many European cities including Vienna, Istanbul, Frankfurt, Amsterdam, and Zurich. From the airport, take a taxi or bus to Sarajevo city centre, then connect to Visoko.
