🕐 Opening Hours

Pyramid of the Sun Dawn to dusk, daily
Ravne Tunnels 9:00 – 17:00
Best season April – October
Winter visits Possible, cold & muddy
Best time: June–September. Early morning avoids crowds and gives the best light.

🎒 What to Bring

👟
Sturdy footwear
Paths are uneven and steep
💧
Water
Minimum 1.5L for the climb
🧥
Light jacket
Tunnels stay ~12°C
☀️
Sunscreen & hat
No shade on the slopes
📷
Camera
Views from the top are stunning
💵
Cash (BAM)
Some vendors don't take cards

📅 Suggested Day Itinerary

9:00
Arrive & head to Pyramid of the Sun
Park at the main entrance, grab a coffee at the nearby café
9:30
Climb the Pyramid of the Sun
~45 min up, 30 min down. Panoramic views at the top
11:30
Lunch in Visoko
Ćevapi, burek, or a sit-down meal — plenty of options in town
13:00
Ravne Underground Tunnels
1–1.5 hour guided tour through the main tunnel network
15:00
Archaeological Park & visitor centre
Museum, exhibitions, and souvenir shop
16:30
Head back or explore Visoko town
Famous for leather goods — great for shopping
