Everything you need to know before you go — tickets, hours, tips, and where to sleep.

🎟️ Tickets & Entrance

Pyramid of the Sun Free
Pyramid of the Moon Free
Ravne Tunnels — Adult ~10 BAM (€5)
Ravne Tunnels — Child ~5 BAM (€2.50)
Archaeological Park Small fee

Prices may vary by season. Check the official foundation website for current rates.

🕐 Opening Hours

Pyramid of the Sun Open daily, dawn to dusk
Ravne Tunnels 9:00 – 17:00 (peak season)
Best season April – October
Winter visits Possible, but cold & muddy
Best time to visit: June–September for warm weather. Early morning visits avoid crowds and offer the best light for photos.

🎒 What to Bring

👟
Sturdy footwear
The paths are uneven and steep
💧
Water
Minimum 1.5L for the pyramid climb
🧥
Light jacket
The tunnels are ~12°C year-round
☀️
Sunscreen & hat
No shade on the pyramid slopes
📷
Camera
The views from the top are stunning
💵
Cash (BAM)
Some vendors don't accept cards

📅 Suggested Day Itinerary

9:00
Arrive & head to Pyramid of the Sun
Park at the main entrance, grab a coffee at the nearby café
9:30
Climb the Pyramid of the Sun
~45 min up, 30 min down. Enjoy the panoramic view at the top
11:30
Lunch in Visoko town
Ćevapi, burek, or a sit-down meal — plenty of options in town
13:00
Ravne Underground Tunnels
1–1.5 hour guided tour through the main tunnel network
15:00
Archaeological Park & visitor centre
Browse the museum, exhibitions, and souvenir shop
16:30
Head back or explore Visoko town
Visoko is famous for its leather goods — great for shopping

🏨 Where to Stay

In Visoko

Several guesthouses and small hotels in town. Staying here puts you steps from the pyramid entrance and lets you explore at your own pace.

~€30–60/night

In Sarajevo

The most popular option. Stay in the beautiful capital and do a day trip to Visoko. Huge range of accommodation from hostels to boutique hotels.

€20–120+/night

Rural Stays

Traditional Bosnian farmhouses and agritourism stays are scattered around the Visoko valley. A magical and authentic experience.

~€40–70/night
